A little bit about me

Born and raised in Brooklyn, New York, I've been working in the digital field for over 3 years. I hold a B.S. in Business Management with a concentration in Marketing from Marist College in Poughkeepsie, New York. I also studied at London South Bank University for 6 months (though I wish I could have stayed longer).

I’m an ambitious team player with excellent communication skills, a high attention to detail, a great work ethic and an ability to thrive in fast paced, challenging environments. I have a passion to create excellent work and am driven to succeed at whatever I do.

When I'm not working, I enjoy powerlifting, reading nonfiction books, jamming out on a few different instruments, snowboarding, travelling, playing videogames and working on my coding and web development skills.
